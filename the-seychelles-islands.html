Welcome to the Seychelles

  

The Seychelles archipelago, located at the Eastern edge of the somalia sea, in the indian Ocean, consists of 116 islands scattered accross its 177 square miles. the Seychelles has a population of 95,000 . The spoken languages are Creole as the mother language, followed by English and French. Most of the Seychelles outer islands are uninhabited, while very few have hotel establishments.

  

There are three major islands which includes Mahe as the main island, as well as Praslin and La Digue. On the island of Mahe, is the capital city named Victoria. Main airport is called the Seychelles International Airport, located at Pointe Larue on Mahe. There is also a smaller airport on Praslin which is a domestic airport.

  

The Seychelles economy is highly dependent on tourism followed by the agricultural sector. The islands offers one of the Mostbeautiful destinations on the planet. According to its National Bureau of Statistics the total number of visitors per year averages to 350,000. Most of its visitors comes from Germany, United kingdom, Northern Ireland, Italy, the United Arab Emirates, and Switzerland.
