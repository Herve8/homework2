Tourist Attractions

  

The best time of the year to visit Seychelles is between July and October where the weather is perfect for outdoor activities. There is a lot of activities to choose from, such as beach hopping. islan hopping, water sports, visiting places, hiking, and also the wonderful island expedition. Ensure that as soon as you arrive at your accomodation, you get your event organiser to organise youroutdoor activities.

  

Regardless on which island that you stay, there;s surely plenty of outdoor adventures for you. If you are a water sports fan, there are plenty of tour operators who organises fishing trips, island excursions, diving, snorkelling, and parachuting.
