Where to stay

  

There is a variety of accomodations available in the form of hotels, guest houses, and self accomodations caterings. Wheteher you want to spend your holidays on the main island of Mahe, or enjoy the tranquility of Praslin or La Digue islands, there's a lot of choices at your disposal.

  

Whether you are residing in a hotel, guest house, or self catering accomodation, you have equal chance to enjoy those delicatesse. If you are residing in a hotel or guest house, those food are already on the menu. If you are residing in a self catering accomodation you can do so by having lunch or dinner at some restaurants who prepares traditional food, especially for visitors.
